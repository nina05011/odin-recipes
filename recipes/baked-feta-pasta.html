Baked Feta Pasta

A delicious, easy-to-make recipe, inspired by Tiktok.

This recipe will have you loving pasta once again! Easy to make, and quick to satisfy any carb craving you may currently be having during this long winter season. All you need is a handful of ingredients, most of which you probably already own. Scroll down below to get the exact details!

Ingredients

  • Feta
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Red Chilli Flakes
  • Oregano
  • Spaghetti

Instructions

  1. Set oven to 400 celcius
  2. Line pan with parchment paper
  3. Add olive oil to pan
  4. Add feta to centre of pan and place cherry tomatoes around it
  5. Add extra olive oil to top of feta
  6. Add on oregano and red chilli flakes to taste
  7. Place in oven for 40 minutes
  8. Boil your pasta
  9. Once 40 minutes have passed, remove pan from oven, mix contents
  10. Add the sauce to your pasta and serve
